⚠️ DO NOT SCAN UNKNOWN QR CODES

Many students have already been victims of Quishing — QR-based phishing attacks.

Digital Safety First

Please read the following precautions carefully to stay protected online.

✅ Do's

  • Verify the sender before scanning any QR code.
  • Always preview the link before clicking through a QR code.
  • Report suspicious activity to a trusted adult or school authority.
  • Keep your devices updated and secure.
  • Use strong passwords and enable 2-factor authentication.

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't scan QR codes from unknown sources or posters.
  • Don't share personal information or passwords online.
  • Don't click on links without checking their authenticity.
  • Don't assume every QR code is safe — pause and think.
  • Don't ignore red flags like fake logins or untrusted redirects.
